About Me

Greetings, I am Cristal Aguilar and I am an animator and illustrator in San Francisco. I love mythical stories and images that intrigues and awakens imagination from the viewer. My goal with my work is to create these reactions. I am also a boundless creator, using mediums that are unusual to use in animation. I believe these different mediums can provoke an emotional reaction that can translate into something new and inspiring.
