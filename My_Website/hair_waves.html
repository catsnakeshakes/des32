Hair Waves

Hair Waves is a Stop Motion Animated Experimental film. It was shot using the software Dragonframe on glass using underhead lighting. Human hair is used as a fluid medium. The film experiments and challenges the flexibility and limitations of human hair. Film was done entirely by myself, except sound.
