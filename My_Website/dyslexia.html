Dyslexia

A Stop Motion Animated film shot with the software Dragonframe on glass using overhead lighting. Black spray painted alphabet pasta is used as a medium to symbolically express dyslexia. Learn about the many symptoms and facts about dyslexia and how it effects people's lives. Film was done entirely by myself, except sound.
